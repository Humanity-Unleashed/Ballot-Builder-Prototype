No moral judgments. No political labels. Just what you prioritize and how you prefer things to work — described so both poles feel like valid, positive preferences.

Design Principles
Both poles are positive
"Broader safety net" and "More personal choice" are both framed as good things. No score is better or worse.
Describes outcomes, not motives
"You want systems with safety nets" — not "you value Care." We don't tell you WHY you want it.
No partisan signal
Nothing maps to left/right, D/R, or liberal/conservative. It's about civic style, not political team.
