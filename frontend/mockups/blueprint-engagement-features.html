Blueprint: Engagement Features

Two identity approaches for the Blueprint's post-assessment view. Both add the same 4 research-backed features (archetype/identity, insight, share, vote CTA) — the difference is how the identity is framed.

What the Research Says to Add
Identity framing — "Being a voter" increased turnout 79% to 89.9%. Give users a civic identity, not just data.
Shareable results — Personality-test-style outputs people want to share. No policy stances in the share card.
Values to action — Connect the blueprint to a voting plan while users are still engaged with their results.
Step 1: Completion Moment
Brief reveal screen after assessment finishes. Shows for 2-3 seconds or until the user taps "See Your Blueprint."
